Color Development Process

The recovery and color development process after lip blushing

Day 1

Just After Treatment

Lips may feel slightly tight due to trauma and numbing; mild burning sensation is normal.

Day 2

Lips Dry & Preparing to Peel

Lips begin to dry; use restorative balm to support natural peeling.

Days 3–5

Lips Peeling, Color Not Yet Even

Peeling is complete; color is light, edges are darker, center is lighter. During this time, frequent care and careful restrictions are needed.

Days 10–20

Lip Color Gradually Stabilizing

Color may be darker or lighter depending on skin type and care. This is a normal stage; no need to worry. Apply balm regularly.

Days 30–40

Fresh, Plump Lips

Lip color begins to even out, lips become more plump and smooth. Continue applying balm regularly.

Days 50–60

Color Fully Stabilized

Lip color is stable; clients can get a touch-up if the color hasn't reached the desired result.
